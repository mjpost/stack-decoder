Instructions

Click on source-language word translations to add them to the chart. Each chart hypothesis contains the words of the hypothesis together a graphical depiction of which words in that hypothesis have been translated.

You can extend hypotheses in two ways: (1) by clicking on the chart item and then selecting a word to add to it, and (2) by dragging target-language word translations onto an hypothesis. Only valid extensions are permitted, e.g., you can't extend a hypothesis using a word translation associated with a source-word that has already been translated in that hypothesis.
